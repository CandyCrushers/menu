Supreme Burrito

A supreme burrito is a hearty, flavorful meal packed with seasoned ground beef or shredded chicken, rice, and beans, wrapped in a warm flour tortilla. It’s topped with fresh salsa, sour cream, shredded cheese, guacamole, and a sprinkle of lettuce for crunch. Customize it with your favorite add-ins like jalapeños or hot sauce for an extra kick!

Preperation Time

  • Total time: 35 Minutes
  • Total time: 15 Minutes
  • Total time: 20 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla (large)
  • Ground beef or shredded chicken (seasoned)
  • Cooked rice
  • Refried beans or black beans
  • Salsa (fresh or mild)
  • Sour cream
  • Shreded cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Lettuce
  • Jalapeños
  • Hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas: Heat the flour tortillas in a skillet over medium heat for about 20-30 seconds on each side to make them soft and pliable.
  2. Prepare the ingredients: Cook the rice, heat the beans, and cook the ground beef or chicken with your preferred seasoning.
  3. Assemble the burrito: Place rice, beans, meat, cheese, salsa, sour cream, guacamole, and shredded lettuce in the center of the tortilla.
  4. Roll the burrito: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  5. Serve: Cut the burrito in half (optional) and serve.
